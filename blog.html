Our Certificates

At IIT Dholakhpur, we take immense pride in the certificates we offer. Our certificates represent a symbol of achievement, recognition, and expertise in various fields of study. Whether it's a certificate of completion for a short-term course or a degree certificate for a comprehensive program, our certificates are a testament to the hard work and dedication of our students. With our rigorous academic curriculum, industry-aligned programs, and esteemed faculty, earning an IIT Dholakhpur certificate holds significant value in the professional world. Our certificates open doors to exciting career opportunities, contribute to personal growth, and pave the way for a successful future. Join us at IIT Dholakhpur and unlock a world of possibilities with our prestigious certificates.


At IIT Dholakhpur, we offer a wide range of prestigious certificates that empower our students with specialized knowledge and skills. Some of the certificates we provide include:

1. Certificate of Completion: Awarded to students who successfully complete short-term courses and workshops, enhancing their expertise in specific areas of interest.

2. Diploma Certificates: Recognizing the successful completion of diploma programs that provide comprehensive training in fields such as Engineering, Business Administration, Information Technology, and more.

3. Undergraduate Degree Certificates: Acknowledging the completion of bachelor's degree programs, showcasing the academic accomplishments and proficiency attained in diverse disciplines.

4. Postgraduate Degree Certificates: Conferred upon students who complete master's degree programs, demonstrating advanced knowledge, research capabilities, and specialized expertise in their chosen fields.

5. Research Certificates: Awarded to students who actively participate in research projects, showcasing their contribution to knowledge generation and innovation.

6. Co-curricular and Extracurricular Certificates: Recognizing students' involvement and achievements in various co-curricular and extracurricular activities, including sports, cultural events, leadership roles, and community service.

Each certificate earned at IIT Dholakhpur carries immense value, signifying the dedication, hard work, and commitment of our students. These certificates act as a gateway to exciting career opportunities and academic pursuits, ensuring our students stand out in their chosen professions.
